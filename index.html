Maxfiylik Siyosati · Privacy Policy

01

Yig'iladigan ma'lumotlar

Farzandim ilovasi quyidagi ma'lumotlarni yig'adi:

  • Geolokatsiya — bolaning joylashuvi, Safe Zone va SOS signal uchun
  • Ekran vaqti — qaysi ilovada qancha vaqt o'tkazganligi (kontent o'qilmaydi)
  • Profil ma'lumotlari — ism, yosh, avatar (ro'yxatdan o'tishda kiritiladi)
  • O'quv faolligi — olimpiada natijalari, o'qilgan kitoblar, XP ballari
  • Qurilma identifikatori — bola va ota-ona qurilmalarini bog'lash uchun
Klaviatura yozuvlari, SMS, qo'ng'iroq tarixi va kontent matnlari hech qachon yig'ilmaydi.
02

Ma'lumotlar qanday ishlatiladi

  • Bolaning xavfsizligini ta'minlash — geolokatsiya va Safe Zone bildirishnomalari
  • Ota-ona nazorat panelida statistika ko'rsatish
  • Olimpiada va o'quv tavsiyalari personalizatsiyasi
  • SOS signali yetkazib berish
  • Ilovaning ishlashini yaxshilash (anonim analytics)
Ma'lumotlar faqat Farzandim platformasi doirasida ishlatiladi. Reklama yoki profiling uchun foydalanilmaydi.
03

Ma'lumotlar uchinchi tomonga berilmaydi

Farzandim foydalanuvchi ma'lumotlarini hech qanday uchinchi tomon kompaniya, reklama tarmog'i yoki davlat organiga sotmaydi va bermaydi.

  • Firebase (Google) — push bildirishnomalar uchun (maxfiylik siyosati bilan himoyalangan)
  • To'lov tizimi — faqat to'lov ma'lumotlari, shaxsiy ma'lumotlar emas
Barcha ma'lumotlar O'zbekiston hududidagi serverlarda saqlanadi (ZRU-547 talabiga muvofiq).
04

Ota-ona nazorati va roziligi

Farzandim — ota-onalar uchun mo'ljallangan nazorat platformasi:

  • Bola ilovani o'rnatishdan oldin ota-ona roziligini talab qiladi
  • Monitoring shaffof — bola qaysi ma'lumotlar yig'ilayotganini ko'rishi mumkin
  • Ota-ona istalgan vaqt nazorat sozlamalarini o'zgartirishi mumkin
  • 13 yoshdan kichik bolalar uchun ota-ona roziligisiz ro'yxatdan o'tib bo'lmaydi
O'zbekiston qonunchiligiga muvofiq: ZRU-547 (Shaxsiy ma'lumotlar), ZRU-444 (Bolalarni himoya).
05

Ma'lumotlarni o'chirish huquqi

Har bir foydalanuvchiga quyidagi huquqlar berilgan:

  • Ko'rish huquqi — o'z ma'lumotlarini so'rab olish
  • O'zgartirish huquqi — profil va sozlamalarni tahrirlash
  • O'chirish huquqi — hisobni va barcha ma'lumotlarni butunlay o'chirish
  • Chiqib ketish huquqi — ilovadan istalgan vaqt chiqib ketish
Hisob o'chirilgandan so'ng 30 kun ichida barcha shaxsiy ma'lumotlar serverlardan o'chiriladi.

01

Data We Collect

Farzandim collects the following information:

  • Geolocation — child's location for Safe Zone alerts and SOS signal
  • Screen time — which apps are used and for how long (content is not read)
  • Profile data — name, age, avatar (entered during registration)
  • Learning activity — olympiad results, books read, XP points
  • Device identifier — to link child and parent devices
Keystrokes, SMS, call logs, and message content are never collected.
02

How We Use Your Data

  • Ensuring child safety — geolocation and Safe Zone notifications
  • Displaying statistics in the parent dashboard
  • Personalizing olympiad and learning recommendations
  • Delivering SOS signals
  • Improving app performance (anonymous analytics)
Data is used solely within the Farzandim platform. It is never used for advertising or profiling.
03

No Third-Party Data Sharing

Farzandim does not sell or share user data with any third-party companies, advertising networks, or government agencies.

  • Firebase (Google) — for push notifications only (covered by their privacy policy)
  • Payment processor — payment data only, no personal information
All data is stored on servers located in Uzbekistan, in compliance with law ZRU-547.
04

Parental Control & Consent

Farzandim is a parental control platform designed for families:

  • Parental consent is required before a child installs the app
  • Monitoring is transparent — children can see what data is collected
  • Parents can modify monitoring settings at any time
  • Children under 13 cannot register without explicit parental consent
Compliant with Uzbekistan law: ZRU-547 (Personal Data) and ZRU-444 (Child Protection).
05

Your Right to Delete Data

Every user is granted the following rights:

  • Right to access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to correct — edit your profile and settings
  • Right to delete — permanently delete your account and all data
  • Right to withdraw — leave the platform at any time
After account deletion, all personal data is removed from our servers within 30 days.
